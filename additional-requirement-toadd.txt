1- For effective visualization and to provide meaningful insights about your products, you can consider adding the following fields to your serializer:

Sales Data: Total sales, sales per month, etc.
Inventory Turnover: How frequently the inventory is sold and replaced.
Average Time in Inventory: How long products stay in inventory before being sold.
Revenue: Total revenue generated by the product.
Customer Demographics: Information about the customers who buy the product (age group, location, etc.)
Return Rate: How often the product is returned.
Top Selling Periods: Best times of the year or month for sales.
